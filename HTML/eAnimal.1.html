The Feeding Habits of Bengal Cats

Click on the button to toggle between showing and hiding content.

A great supplement of the Bengal cats diet is raw meat that is fresh and not processed. You can also make your own cat food or buy cat food from the store. If you plan to buy or make their food, make sure it comtains 50% to 80% of protein in it. The rest of the diet should contain fibers and vitamins.
