Soccer

I have won 2 city championships while playing soccer. When I used to play for Kilmer, we won the city championships. I was in 5th grade and it was my first time playing for a school team. I did not play a lot because most of the team was 6th graders and I was only in 5th grade. I was still really happy when we won and my coach decided to play me the whole game during the finals. The 2nd time I won city championships was in 8th grade. This time I was way more experienced and had played more throughout the season. This, however, didn't change the feeling I felt when I realized I won. I was more nervous this time though because the game was a tie and we had to go into penalty shootouts. My team was also a runner up when I was in 7th grade and I was devastated when I realized we had lost.
